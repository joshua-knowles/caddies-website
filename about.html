About

The caddie behind Caddies.

Josh is the founder, owner and operator of Caddies. He shows up, sets everything up and runs the simulator from start to finish. Every event, every time.

He started Caddies because he loves golf and wanted more people to experience it. Not everyone has the time, the membership or the handicap to get out on a course. But put a simulator in front of almost anyone and something clicks. They want another shot. Then another. That's the feeling he's here to create for your guests.

Caddies is a one-person operation. When you book, you deal with Josh from the first email to the last putt. He's the one who replies, shows up, runs the day and packs it all away. No contractors, no handoffs, no surprises.
