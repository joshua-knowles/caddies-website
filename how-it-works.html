How it works

Easier than you think.

Here's what happens from the first enquiry to the final hole.

01

Get in touch

Fill out our enquiry form. Tell us your date, venue and roughly what you're after. We'll come back to you fast.

02

We sort the details

We'll confirm the package, answer any questions and lock in your tee time. No back-and-forth. No complicated contracts.

03

We show up

On the day, we arrive early and set up the full simulator in under an hour. Everything is ready before your guests walk in.

04

You enjoy the event

We run the simulator throughout. Guests just pick up a club and play. We handle everything else.

05

We pack down and leave

When the event wraps up, we have everything packed and out the door in under an hour. The venue is back to normal before you know it.

Requirements

What you need to provide

Just the space. We bring everything else.

  • Minimum floor area of 17x17ft
  • At least 10ft of ceiling clearance
  • Access to a standard power outlet (no power? We can bring a generator)
